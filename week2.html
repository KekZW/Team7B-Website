Partners Meeting

We showcase our high-fidelity prototypes and gather feedback to the partners.

Question Partner's Response
What do you think about this prototype? It was good, but we should consider why people would want to use this application and how we should advertise it.
Is there any features we should consider? Maybe a few more features, like news updates and community links to the FORK

We also have been asked a few questions regarding the data.

Partner's Question Response
Where did you get this data? It was from the official Scottish Environmental Protection Agency (SEPA) data, but the issue is that it is outdated by 2 years

Main Insights

Key insights from the partner meeting.

  • Community: Even though the app is, feature-wise, good, it is important for the app to have a community or links to the FORK community to allow for engagement.
  • FORK Awareness: It is important for the app to have a link or news section to keep users updated about the FORK community and its activities.

Integrative Thinking

During this stage, we had a few ideas and also concerns. This is in regards to the login system, where we had two different ideas:

  • Login System: We force users to create an account to use the app, which would allow us to gather more data and also allow users to save their preferences.
  • Guest System: We allow users to use the app without creating an account, which would allow for a more seamless experience but would limit our ability to gather data and save preferences.

We decided to look into both, mainly on the pros os each. And we were able to find the middle ground through active discussion, where we allow the app to be mostly account free, only when it is with regards to report, we require them to login.

Usability Testing

We conducted usability testing on our classmates to gather feedback on the prototypes.

  • Confusing Label: The label are confusing, there should be a way for us to know what each of them means
  • Clipping Images: Some of the charts were clipping through the labels.
  • Unusable QR Code: Since this is a mobile app, there should be a way to click into the community tabs as there is no way for them to scan the QR code.
